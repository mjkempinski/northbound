How we work

A repeatable operating system, not ad hoc consulting

Every engagement runs on the same four artifacts — built and refined on real payroll change, not written for a proposal.

A

Payroll change intake form

  • Business reason
  • Legal & compliance impact
  • Payroll countries affected
  • Upstream / downstream systems
  • Required effective date
  • Rollback requirements
B

Impact assessment template

  • Configuration changes
  • Interface changes
  • Reporting changes
  • Testing scope
  • Risk rating — low / medium / high
C

Test pack

  • Parallel payroll
  • Retro calculations
  • New joiner / leaver scenarios
  • Overtime & allowance cases
  • Statutory calculations
  • GL posting validation
D

Cutover checklist

  • Config migrated
  • Interfaces enabled
  • Security validated
  • Payroll control totals signed off
  • Rollback plan approved
Who this is for

Built for the person who owns the change

A good fit if

  • You own or influence a payroll, HRIS or workforce system change — Head of Payroll, Payroll Director, Head of HR Systems, HR Transformation Lead, or Director of People Operations
  • Your organisation has roughly 500–5,000 employees
  • You have a change on the roadmap — new system, vendor switch, M&A payroll harmonisation, or multi-country consolidation
  • You want an accountable delivery partner, not another vendor to manage

Probably not yet a fit if

  • You're looking for a payroll bureau to run payroll on an ongoing basis
  • You need enterprise-wide strategy consulting or target operating model design
  • You're a very small business without a dedicated payroll or HR systems function

Questions

Frequently asked

Are you a new practice? How much experience do you have?

Northbound is an early-stage practice. It grew out of hands-on payroll change delivery work inside Wagestream, not years of consultancy trading. If you need a large bench of consultants with a decade of case studies, we're probably not the right fit yet. If you want senior, hands-on attention from someone who has actually run this work, we might be.

Do you run our payroll for us, or support the change?

We're a change delivery partner, not a payroll bureau. We don't process your payroll on an ongoing basis — we help you scope, test, cut over and stabilise a change to your payroll or HR systems, then hand back a working, documented process.

How do you handle UK payroll compliance — HMRC, RTI, BACS, auto-enrolment?

Our test packs and cutover checklists are built around statutory calculation testing, RTI (FPS/EPS) readiness, BACS validation and pension auto-enrolment settings, alongside GL posting and control total sign-off, so compliance checks are part of the delivery method rather than an afterthought.

Can you help if we're already mid-project and things are going wrong?

Yes. Bringing in structured intake, an honest impact assessment and a proper test pack partway through a struggling implementation is one of the most common ways this kind of support gets used. We can join at any stage, not just at the start.

What does this cost?

It depends on the scope of the change. We start with a free 30-minute conversation to understand what's coming up, then propose a fixed-fee scope for the engagement — no open-ended day-rate billing without a defined outcome.

Which payroll or HR systems do you work with?

We work with whatever's relevant to your change rather than claiming coverage across every platform on the market. Tell us your systems on the first call and we'll tell you honestly whether we're the right fit.
